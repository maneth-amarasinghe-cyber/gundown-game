HOW TO PLAY

Find every hidden target.

Targets are randomly hidden in the numbered grid. Click different cells to make guesses. A successful guess becomes ✓ Found. When you finish, missed targets are revealed.

The browser version keeps the original Gundown rules: grid sizes from 3×3 to 5×5, and between 2 and half of the total cells as hidden targets.
